### Sample Insights
- Women are more likely to buy compared to men (~65%)  
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 states  
- Adult age group (30–49 yrs) is max contributing (~50%)  
- Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra channels are max contributing

### Final Conclusion to improve Vrinda store sales
Target **women** customers of age group **30–49 yrs** living in **Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh** by showing ads/offers/coupons available on **Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra**.

---

## Short Summary of Overall Analysis
- **Gender:** Women account for the majority of purchases (~65%).  
- **Age:** 30–49 years is the largest contributing group (~50%).  
- **Geography:** Top states are Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh.  
- **Channels:** Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra drive the most sales.  

**Recommendation:** Focus marketing and promotional campaigns on women aged 30–49 in MH/KA/UP and run targeted offers/ads on the top channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra) to increase conversion.
